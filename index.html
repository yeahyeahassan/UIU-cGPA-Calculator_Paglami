Theory and Lab Course Structure

Our academic structure for courses follows a detailed assessment plan, ensuring that students are comprehensively evaluated across multiple areas of performance.

Theory Courses (3 Credits)

The total marks for theory courses are distributed as follows:

  • Assignments: 5 marks
  • Attendance: 5 marks
  • Midterm Exam: 30 marks
  • Final Exam: 40 marks
  • Class Tests/Quizzes: 20 marks (Best 3 out of 4 quizzes are considered, and their average is taken)

Pass Marks: Students must achieve a minimum of 55 out of 100 marks to pass the course.

Lab Courses (1 Credit)

Lab courses are assessed based on practical performance and report submissions. These are typically structured around hands-on experiments and are valued at 1 credit per course.
